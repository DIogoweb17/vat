VAT Calculator instructions

The VAT Calculator allows you to quickly add or remove VAT from any amount.
Simply enter the amount, select whether you want to **add** or **exclude** VAT,
enter the VAT rate, and press "Calculate" or hit "Enter".
The tool will instantly show the VAT amount and the final total.
Perfect for businesses, freelancers, and anyone needing quick VAT calculations.
